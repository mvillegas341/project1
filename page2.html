The Inspirations

Claude Monet

Claude Monet wasa French artist, known as one of the founders of French impressionist art. Known for painting images of controlled nature as well as spearheading an art style revolving around a focus in texture. Some of his most famous pieces are Impression,Sunrise, The Water Lily Pond, andWoman with a Parasol- Madame Monet and Her Son. You can learn more about Claude Monet here!

Junji Ito

Junji Ito is a Mangaka from Japan known for his unique and popular horror stories. They have a focus on body horror, cosmic horror,and the grotesque. His most popular works are Uzumaki, as well as Tomie.Although he has published many more. You can learn more about Junji Ito here!

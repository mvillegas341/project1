About Me!

My name is Michael Villegas and i'm a IT major at USF, there really is no good reason for me to have this art portfolio as I am not an art major at all I just draw sometimes and thought it'd be fun. I started mainly drawing after the initial COVID summer and I had in person class again and needed a way to pass the time on campus. After liking it I decided to take a couple of art classes at USF to fill in some extra credit hours I needed and the rest is history.
